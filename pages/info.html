    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Bidder is a 100% independent company, have a safe and secure payment solution for both buyer and seller.

    Secure payment is made to our client account at DNB, and settlement is carried out by our settlement department, which currently has 15 employees, of which 3 are auditors.

    You as a bidder should feel secure when you submit a bid. As a buyer, you always have access to the bid log. The bidders are anonymised, for the sake of the bidders. If you wish to have full access to the bid log, contact one of our employees. IP addresses are registered on all bids.

    How can I register at Bidder?

    Head over to the register page, and follow the instructions.

    I get an error while registering?

    Normally, error messages give information that there is missing contact information in the registration. Remember to confirm with your password. If you continue to receive the same error message, please open a new internet window and try again. Email and password are written consecutively, without spaces or hyphens. The password must be at least 8 characters long.

    I get a message that my account is blocked?

    A blocking of an account is normally due to previous violations of rules and conditions, or that we have incorrect contact information for you. Please send an e-mail to post@bidder.zxc for more information.

    How old do I have to be to shop at Bidder?

    You must be over 18 years of age to participate in auctions on Bidder. We will cancel orders where it appears that the bid has been placed by a person under the age of 18.

    How do I find out more about an object?

    You will find detailed information for each object. For larger and more images, click on the image. You can also read the technical condition report and certification, if the object has this. If you have specific questions about an object, and the information has not been provided, questions can be sent directly to the seller listed in the auction.
